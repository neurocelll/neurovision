About Neurovision

Neurovision is a simple-to-use text extractor created by Neurocell. It utilizes Tesseract.js to perform Optical Character Recognition (OCR) directly in the browser, enabling easy extraction of text from images.

With Neurovision, you can effortlessly upload an image and extract any readable text it contains. Whether you're working with scanned documents, screenshots, or photos, Neurovision brings the power of OCR to your fingertips.
